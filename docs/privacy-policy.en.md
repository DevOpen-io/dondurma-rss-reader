# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-22

This Privacy Policy explains how Dondurma RSS Reader processes information when you use the app.

## Summary

We do not create user accounts. We do not sell personal data. We do not use analytics SDKs or advertising SDKs. Most app data stays on your device.

## Information We Process

We process information you choose to add to the app, including:

- RSS or Atom feed URLs you subscribe to
- Feed names, categories, and folder labels you set
- Bookmarks you save
- Read or unread state
- Search history and excluded keywords
- App preferences such as theme, language, sync interval, notification settings, browser mode, cache limit, and font settings

If you enable full-text extraction, the app may also fetch the original article URL for a feed item.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- Fetch and display feed content
- Organize feeds, bookmarks, and categories
- Save reading state and preferences on your device
- Show local notifications for new items when enabled
- Let you export or import subscriptions through OPML

## Local Storage

App data is stored locally on your device using Hive CE. The main local data stores include settings, feed data, and bookmarks.

This local data can include subscriptions, cached article content, bookmarked items, read IDs, custom categories, and preference values.

## Network Requests

The app makes network requests only to provide app features you use. These may include:

- RSS or Atom feed sources you add
- Original article pages, if full-text extraction is enabled
- A suggested feeds list hosted on GitLab
- Google Favicon service for feed icons
- Google Fonts service for font assets

When the app fetches feeds or article pages, the source website may receive your IP address, user agent, and request time. Those services may log requests according to their own policies.

## Notifications

The app can show local notifications on your device. Notifications are handled by your operating system and the app does not send notification data to a server.

Notification content may include article information needed to open the item you tapped.

## In-App Browser and External Links

The app can open links in a built-in browser, an external browser, or the system browser depending on your settings.

When you open a third-party website, that website may collect data according to its own privacy policy.

## Sharing and Files

The app can use the system share sheet to share links or articles. It can also import or export OPML files. These actions stay on your device unless you choose to share the content elsewhere.

## Data Retention and Control

Your data stays on your device until you delete it. You can remove data by using app controls such as:

- Clear cache
- Clear search history
- Reset app data
- Remove subscriptions or bookmarks

If you uninstall the app, locally stored app data is normally removed by the operating system.

## Children

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect local app data, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
