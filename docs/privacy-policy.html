🍦 Dondurma RSS Reader

Privacy Policy — Last updated: June 2026

ℹ️ Introduction

Dondurma RSS Reader ("the App") is developed by DevOpen (Talha Aksoy & Eren Gün). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

💾 Data Collection & Storage

The App stores all data locally on your device using Hive database. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Data stored locally includes:

  • RSS feed subscriptions (URLs and names)
  • Cached article content for offline reading
  • Bookmarked articles
  • App settings and preferences
  • Search history

☁️ No Server Communication

The App does not have its own backend server. All RSS feeds are fetched directly from the original source websites. We do not act as an intermediary and do not log, monitor, or store your reading activity.

🧩 Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Outfit font family)
  • WebView — for displaying web content within the app
  • Ad Blocker (adblocker_webview) — optional ad filtering in the built-in browser

These services may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

🔔 Notifications

The App may send local notifications for new articles. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external push notification service. You can disable notifications at any time in Settings.

🔒 Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security. We recommend using device-level security measures such as screen lock and encryption.

👶 Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App and the updated date will be changed accordingly.
